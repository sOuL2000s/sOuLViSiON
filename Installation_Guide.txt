sOuLViSiON Installation Instructions (PWA Simulation)

Since this app is optimized for client-side use, you can "install" it without a traditional app store:

FOR PC (Chrome / Edge / Brave):
1. Navigate to your hosted website URL.
2. Click the 'Three Dots' (Menu) in the top right corner.
3. Hover over 'Save and Share' or 'More Tools'.
4. Select 'Install sOuLViSiON' or 'Create Shortcut'.
5. Check 'Open as window' and click Create/Install.
   - The website will now appear as a standalone app in your Taskbar and Start Menu.

FOR IPHONE (Safari):
1. Open the website in Safari.
2. Tap the 'Share' icon (square with upward arrow) at the bottom.
3. Scroll down and tap 'Add to Home Screen'.
4. Confirm by tapping 'Add'.
   - The logo.svg will appear as your app icon on your home screen.

FOR ANDROID (Chrome):
1. Open the website in Chrome.
2. Tap the 'Three Dots' (Menu) in the top right.
3. Tap 'Add to Home screen' or 'Install app'.
4. Follow the prompts to confirm.
   - The app will now be available in your App Drawer.
